K. Krishna

A public servant • Community leader • Inspiring public speaker
Summary: K. Krishna rose from a humble farming background, started his career as a bus conductor then with determination and dedication he became a decorated Traffic Inspector and community leader who dedicates himself to social service.

Quick facts

Full name: K. Krishna
Early life: Born to a farming family; grew up with limited resources but great determination.
Profession: Traffic Inspector
Values: Follows Ambedkar’s ideology; prioritises education, dignity, and service.

Early life

K. Krishna was born in a small village to loving parents who worked tirelessly in the fields. From childhood, he learned the dignity of labour and the importance of knowledge.

Career & public service

As a Traffic Inspector, Krishna served the public with dedication and earned several accolades — including being selected as "Best Traffic Inspector" many times.

Beyond his duties, he initiated distributions of books, uniforms, and essentials to those in need.
